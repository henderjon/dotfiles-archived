about

Jon Henderson is a self-trained developer whose hobby turned into something more. Currently the Software Engineering Manager at myON, he oversees the dev team, the code base, and the day to day ins-and-outs of the dev process for an education based SAAS reading platform that serves an ever growing number of books to hundreds of thousands of students around the world every day. He enjoys learning, but enjoys his family more. He currently lives in Roseville with his wife Jessie, twin sons Walker and Davis, and his dog Goose where he spends most of his free time cooking, reading, going on walks, and training to be a curmudgeon.
