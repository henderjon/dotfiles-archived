about

Jon Henderson is a self-trained developer whose hobby turned into something more. Currently the Application Development Manager at myON, he oversees the development team for an education based SAAS reading ecosystem that serves an ever growing number of books to hundreds of thousands of students around the world every day. He enjoys learning, but enjoys his family more. He currently lives in Roseville MN with his wife Jessie, twin sons Walker and Davis, and his dog Goose where he spends most of his free time cooking, reading, going on walks, and training to be a curmudgeon.
